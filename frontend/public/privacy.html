Privacy & Data Protection

This application queries the official SAPS servers and displays only the status returned from SAPS.

What we collect

  • Nothing
  • No personal data is stored on the server side.

Logging

The proxy may log technical information (timestamps, status codes) to help debugging. It does not log reference numbers or serials.
